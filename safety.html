Hiking Safety

Safety should always come first. Arizona’s landscapes are breathtaking but can be dangerous if you’re unprepared.

Top Safety Tips

  • Tell someone your hiking plan and expected return.
  • Carry plenty of water. Dehydration is the #1 danger.
  • Check the weather — monsoon storms can appear fast.
  • Watch for wildlife: snakes, scorpions, and coyotes.
  • Stick to marked trails and respect trail signs.
