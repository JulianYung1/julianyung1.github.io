Popular Trails

Arizona offers a variety of hiking trails for all skill levels. Whether you’re seeking canyon views, red rock formations, or waterfall oases, there’s a trail waiting for you.

Trail Name Difficulty Length (miles)
Bright Angel Trail Moderate 12
Cathedral Rock Hard 1.5
Havasu Falls Moderate 10

Trail Highlights

Bright Angel Trail: Descend into the Grand Canyon with stunning views and multiple rest stops. Watch for changing weather conditions.

Cathedral Rock: A short but steep climb in Sedona with panoramic red rock views. Early mornings are best to avoid crowds.

Havasu Falls: Famous for its turquoise waterfalls. Permits required. Bring plenty of water and plan for an overnight stay.
